
Features of the System
1. Generates groups for class project.
2. Can retrieve fundamental information or allow manual input of information.
3. Collects instructor’s and student’s information and preferences with regards to grouping.
4. Offers option for instructor to approve or modify final group listing.
5. Ensures Instructor’s preferences override student’s preferences where such conflicts arise.
6. Prompts instructor and student to enter their information and preferences.
7. Provides the option to edit previously entered information for both instructor and students.
8. Highlights conflicts that could not be resolved during group generation so that such conflicts are clear to instructor.
9. Notifies students of group listing once finalized by instructor.
10. System accounts for student’s schedules and preferences.
11. Capable of balancing groups in terms of skill for some projects given the students submit additional information.
12. Handle creation of Projects.
